Use Case

You have filled your board with your precious ideas in a Mind Map.

Now, you realize you should take advantages of miro cards properties like

  • assigning a date (could be a deadline)
  • assigning a team member
  • writing detailed description
  • use them in your kanban framework.
But manually creating each cards with the same content as each node of Mind Map would take lot of your time and effort.

Cardsy will do that for you in a single click.

Supported Widgets:

  • Texts (Nodes of Mind Map)

Steps:-

  1. Select nodes of MindMap widgets from your board.
  2. Click on in the shown menu.
  3. That's it! All the cards are created.
